<p><ul><li>Suspension Glass Insulators</li><li>Pin Glass Insulators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Glass Insulator Market comprises two main types - Suspension Glass Insulators and Pin Glass Insulators. Suspension Glass Insulators are designed to hang power lines and support the conductors. They are typically bell-shaped with grooves to hold the wire in place. Pin Glass Insulators, on the other hand, are used to secure the power lines to the tops of utility poles. They have a threaded metal pin that screws into the pole and a groove to hold the wire. Both types play crucial roles in insulating electricity and preventing power loss.</p></p>
