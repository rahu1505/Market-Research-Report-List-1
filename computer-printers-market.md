<p><ul><li>Ink Jet Printers</li><li>Laser Printers</li><li>Dot Matrix Printers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The computer printers market consists of three main types: inkjet printers, laser printers, and dot matrix printers. Inkjet printers function by spraying tiny droplets of ink onto the paper, producing high-quality color images and documents at a relatively low cost. Laser printers employ a laser beam to create electrostatic charges on a drum, which then transfers toner onto the paper, resulting in speedy, high-quality black and white printing. Dot matrix printers use a matrix of tiny pins to strike an inked ribbon against the paper, making them suitable for printing multiple copies of documents with carbon copies.</p></p>
