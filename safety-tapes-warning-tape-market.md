<p><p>Safety tapes, also known as warning tapes, are widely used in various industries and public spaces to indicate potential hazards and promote safety awareness. There are three main types of safety tapes available in the market - PVC, BOPP, and OPP. PVC (polyvinyl chloride) tapes are flexible and durable, making them suitable for rough surfaces. BOPP (biaxially-oriented polypropylene) tapes are waterproof and resistant to chemicals, making them ideal for outdoor applications. OPP (oriented polypropylene) tapes are economical and provide good adhesion, making them commonly used for general-purpose safety marking.</p></p>
