There are two main types of fused alumina: white fused alumina and brown fused alumina. White fused alumina is known for its high purity and hardness, making it suitable for precision grinding and ceramic applications. On the other hand, brown fused alumina has a lower purity level but exhibits higher toughness and strength, making it ideal for abrasive applications like sandpaper and abrasive belts.</p></p>
