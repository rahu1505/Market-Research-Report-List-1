<p><p>The automotive in-wheel motor market consists of two types: outer rotor type and inner rotor type. In the outer rotor type, the motor's rotor is located on the outer part of the motor, which allows it to have a larger diameter and provide higher torque output. On the other hand, the inner rotor type has its rotor placed inside the motor, resulting in a smaller diameter and lower torque output compared to the outer rotor type. Each type offers different advantages and is suitable for varying automotive applications.</p></p>
