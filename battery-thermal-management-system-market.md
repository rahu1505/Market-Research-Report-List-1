<p><p>The Battery Thermal Management System (BTMS) market offers two primary types of cooling and heating solutions: liquid cooling and heating, and air cooling and heating. Liquid cooling and heating systems use a specialized fluid to absorb and dissipate heat generated by batteries, enhancing their performance and lifespan. On the other hand, air cooling and heating systems rely on air circulation to cool down or warm up batteries. Both methods aim to regulate battery temperature and prevent overheating, ensuring safe and efficient operation.</p></p>
