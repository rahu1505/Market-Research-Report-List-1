BHTS offer the advantage of lower cooling costs compared to traditional superconductors, which rely on expensive cryogenic cooling.</p><p>Furthermore, the increasing emphasis on renewable energy sources, such as wind and solar power, is expected to fuel the demand for BHTS in the coming years.
