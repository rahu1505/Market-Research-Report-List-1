<p><p>Extruded Polystyrene (XPS) panels are a type of foam insulation material that is widely used in the construction industry. XPS panels are made from polystyrene, a thermoplastic polymer, through the process of extrusion. These panels have a closed-cell structure, which provides excellent thermal insulation properties and high compressive strength. They are commonly used in roofing, flooring, insulation systems, and wall insulation.</p><p>The market analysis of the Extruded Polystyrene (XPS) Panels market shows a positive outlook and strong growth potential.
