The market for these catalysts can be categorized into three types based on pressure levels: low pressure, medium pressure, and high pressure. Low pressure catalysts are used in methanol synthesis at pressures below 50 atmospheres, medium pressure catalysts are used at pressures between 50 and 100 atmospheres, and high pressure catalysts are employed at pressures above 100 atmospheres. Each type of catalyst is designed to optimize the reaction conditions for efficient methanol synthesis at the specified pressure levels.</p></p>
