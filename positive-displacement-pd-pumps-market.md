The Reciprocating PD pumps use a piston or a diaphragm to create suction and discharge pressure. Rotary PD pumps use rotating mechanisms, such as gears, lobes, or screws, to move the fluid. Other types of PD pumps include peristaltic and progressing cavity pumps. These pumps provide precise and accurate flow rates, making them suitable for applications in various industries such as oil and gas, chemicals, and food processing.</p></p>
