<p><p>The agricultural chelated fertilizer market consists of various types, including EDTA, EDDHA, DTPA, IDHA, and others. EDTA chelated fertilizers contain ethylenediaminetetraacetic acid, which helps in nutrient absorption and utilization by plants. EDDHA chelated fertilizers contain ethylenediamine-N,N'-bis(2-hydroxyphenylacetic acid)- a potent iron chelating agent. DTPA chelated fertilizers contain diethylenetriaminepentaacetic acid and are effective in preventing iron deficiencies in plants. IDHA chelated fertilizers contain iminodisuccinic acid and are known for their high stability and bioavailability. Other types of chelated fertilizers include HEDTA, EDDHSA, and others, each with unique properties and benefits for plant growth.</p></p>
