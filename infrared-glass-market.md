<p><p>Infrared glass is a specialized type of glass that is designed to transmit infrared radiation while blocking visible light. With the increasing demand for thermal imaging systems, infrared glass is becoming a crucial component in industries like aerospace, automotive, and security. The ability of infrared glass to transmit infrared radiation while blocking visible light makes it ideal for thermal imaging cameras and sensors, which are widely used in surveillance, medical, and industrial applications.</p><p>In addition, the growing concern for energy efficiency is driving the use of infrared glass in building construction. Infrared glass coatings are used to control heat transfer and reduce energy consumption in windows and facades, thereby enhancing the sustainability of buildings.</p><p>The future of the infrared glass market looks promising.
