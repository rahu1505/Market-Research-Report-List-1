<p><p>The midrange speaker market consists of three main types: cone types, dome types, and compression horn types. Cone types use a cone-shaped diaphragm to produce sound, offering good clarity and power handling. Dome types, on the other hand, have a dome-shaped diaphragm that provides improved frequency response and accuracy. Compression horn types employ a compression driver and horn to amplify sound, making them suitable for large venues and outdoor events. Each type offers different benefits and is selected based on specific audio requirements and usage scenarios.</p></p>
