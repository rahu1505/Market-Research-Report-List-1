<p><p>SLA 3D printers are a type of 3D printers that use a process called stereolithography to create objects. There are two main types of SLA 3D printers in the market - wax-based and polymer resin-based. Wax-based SLA printers use a combination of wax and a photopolymer to build objects layer by layer. These printers are commonly used in the jewelry industry to create intricate designs. On the other hand, polymer resin-based SLA printers use liquid polymer resin that solidifies when exposed to UV light. These printers are versatile and can be used in various industries, including automotive, healthcare, and consumer goods.</p></p>
