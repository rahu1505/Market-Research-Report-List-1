<p><strong>The Marine Trenchers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mechanical Trenchers</li><li>Jet Trenchers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Marine trenchers are used for underwater excavation and installation of pipelines or cables. Mechanical trenchers are equipped with a rotating digging chain or a cutting wheel to excavate the seabed, while jet trenchers use high-pressure water jets for soil removal. Mechanical trenchers are ideal for firm or rocky seabeds, whereas jet trenchers are used for soft or loose soils. Each type has its advantages and is selected based on the specific requirements of the marine project, such as soil conditions, water depth, and installation specifications.</p></p>
