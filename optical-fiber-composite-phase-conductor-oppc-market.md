<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Optical Fiber Composite Phase Conductor (OPPC) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Central Tube Structure OPPC</li><li>Layer Stranding Structure OPPC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Optical Fiber Composite Phase Conductor (OPPC) market includes two types: Central Tube Structure OPPC and Layer Stranding Structure OPPC. The Central Tube Structure OPPC consists of a central tube where optical fibers are placed, surrounded by layers of aluminum-based conductors. This design allows for efficient power transmission and simultaneous data transfer. On the other hand, the Layer Stranding Structure OPPC has optical fibers embedded within the layers of aluminum-based conductors. Both types offer high capacity and superior performance for power transmission and communication applications.</p></p>
