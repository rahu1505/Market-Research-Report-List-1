<p><p>The rearview mirror replacement market is divided into two types: CCD camera and CMOS camera. CCD camera technology uses a sensor that converts light into electrical signals, providing high-quality images with superior color accuracy but requiring more power. On the other hand, CMOS camera technology offers a lower power consumption, but it may sacrifice some image quality. Both types provide rear-view functionality for vehicles, allowing drivers to have a clear view of the rear surroundings while driving, enhancing safety and convenience.</p></p>
