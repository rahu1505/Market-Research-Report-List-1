<p><ul><li>Carbon Chain Polymer</li><li>Hetero Chain Polymer</li><li>Element Organic Polymer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Polyketone resin is classified into three types based on carbon chain polymer, hetero chain polymer, and element organic polymer. Carbon chain polymers are derived from the polymerization of carbon monoxide and are known for their excellent heat resistance and chemical stability. Hetero chain polymers are formed by combining carbon monoxide with other compounds such as ethylene or propylene oxide, resulting in enhanced flexibility and impact resistance. Element organic polymers are produced by incorporating elements like nitrogen or silicon into the polymer structure, offering unique properties including flame retardancy and electrical conductivity.</p></p>
