<p><p>Shafted screw conveyors are widely used in various industries for material handling. They can be installed in three ways. Horizontal installation involves placing the conveyor in a straight line, facilitating movement of materials in a horizontal direction. Inclined installation sees the conveyor placed at an angle, allowing materials to be transported upwards or downwards. Vertical installation involves positioning the conveyor vertically, enabling the movement of materials in an upward or downward direction. These installation types cater to different needs and requirements of industries depending on the desired direction of material flow.</p></p>
