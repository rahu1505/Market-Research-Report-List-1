<p><p>In-vehicle camera CMOS market has two types: Parallel Type and Serial Type. The Parallel Type refers to a CMOS camera that captures and transfers image data to the processing unit simultaneously using multiple data lines. This type offers high-speed data transfer and is suitable for applications with higher bandwidth requirements. On the other hand, the Serial Type captures and transfers image data sequentially on a single data line. This type is cost-effective and suitable for applications with relatively lower bandwidth requirements. Both types cater to different needs and offer their own advantages.</p></p>
