<p><strong>The Airplane Bearing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Roller</li><li>Ball</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The airplane bearing market can be categorized into two main types: roller bearings and ball bearings. Roller bearings consist of cylindrical rollers, arranged in a row, which helps distribute the load evenly. They are suitable for applications that require heavy radial and thrust loads. On the other hand, ball bearings use steel balls to minimize friction between the bearing races, making them suitable for high-speed and low-load applications. Both roller and ball bearings play vital roles in ensuring smooth and efficient operations of various aircraft systems.</p></p>
