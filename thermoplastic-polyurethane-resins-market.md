<p><p>Thermoplastic Polyurethane (TPU) resins have two main types: aromatic and aliphatic. Aromatic TPU resins are derived from aromatic diisocyanates and offer excellent mechanical properties, high abrasion resistance, and good chemical resistance. They are commonly used in applications like automotive parts, cables, and footwear. On the other hand, aliphatic TPU resins are derived from aliphatic diisocyanates and are known for their exceptional light stability and weather resistance. They find applications in outdoor products, such as films, coatings, and pipes. Both types of TPU resins provide valuable qualities for various industrial applications.</p></p>
