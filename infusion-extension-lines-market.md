<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>Infusion extension lines are categorized into four types based on the number of channels they possess. One-way extension lines have a single channel, usually used for delivering medications or fluids. Two-way extension lines have two channels, allowing for the administration of multiple medications simultaneously. Three-way extension lines consist of three channels, enabling the delivery of fluids, medications, and blood products concurrently. Finally, four-way extension lines are equipped with four channels, offering the flexibility to administer various medications, fluids, and blood products simultaneously. These different types cater to specific medical requirements and enhance the efficiency of infusion therapies.</p></p>
