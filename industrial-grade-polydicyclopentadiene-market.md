There are different types of markets for PDCPD, including Reactive Injection Molding (RIM), Resin Transfer Molding (RTM), Vacuum Assisted Forming (VARI), and Other markets. RIM involves injecting PDCPD into a mold where it reacts and solidifies, forming complex shapes. RTM uses a closed mold and a two-step process to infuse PDCPD into reinforcing fibers. VARI utilizes vacuum pressure to shape PDCPD into desired forms. Other markets include applications where PDCPD is used in various manufacturing processes beyond RIM, RTM, and VARI.</p></p>
