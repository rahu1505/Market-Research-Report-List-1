<p><p>The Cosmetic Preservative Blends market can be categorized into various types, including Parabens, Formaldehyde, Halogenated compounds, Alcohols, Organic Acids, and Others. Formaldehyde, although less favored due to potential health concerns, is still used in some cosmetic products. Halogenated compounds, such as chlorhexidine, are effective against a broad spectrum of microorganisms. Alcohols like ethanol and isopropanol have antimicrobial properties, making them widely used preservatives. Organic acids, like benzoic acid, are also used for their preservative properties. Lastly, the "Others" category includes preservatives such as phenoxyethanol and hexamidine diisethionate.</p></p>
