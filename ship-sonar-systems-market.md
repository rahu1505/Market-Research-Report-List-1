<p><p>Ship sonar systems are classified into three types: long range, middle range, and short distance. Long range sonar systems are designed to detect objects at a vast distance from the ship, enabling early detection of potential threats. Middle range sonar systems have a shorter detection range but offer better accuracy and detail. Short distance sonar systems are used primarily for navigation and obstacle avoidance, with a limited range but high-resolution imaging capabilities. These different types cater to various needs in ship operations, ensuring safety and efficient navigation.</p></p>
