<p><p>The Creatine Kinase Isoenzyme Test Kit market includes two types: Time-resolved Immunofluorescence and Chemiluminescence Immunoassay. In Time-resolved Immunofluorescence, a fluorescent label is added to the test sample, and the emitted light is measured to determine the presence of the enzyme. Chemiluminescence Immunoassay utilizes a chemical reaction that produces light when an enzyme reacts with a substrate, and the intensity of the light is measured to detect the enzyme. Both techniques are used to analyze Creatine Kinase Isoenzymes and provide valuable insights for diagnosis and monitoring of certain medical conditions.</p></p>
