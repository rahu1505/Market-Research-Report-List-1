<p><strong>The Polypropylene Rope Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Blue Split Film Polypropylene Rope</li><li>Yarn Construction Polypropylene Rope</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Polypropylene rope is categorized into two main types - blue split film polypropylene rope and yarn construction polypropylene rope. Blue split film polypropylene rope is made by splitting the polypropylene film into thin strips and then twisting and weaving them together to form a rope. Yarn construction polypropylene rope, on the other hand, is made by spinning individual polypropylene fibers into yarns and then braiding or twisting these yarns together. Both types of polypropylene ropes are widely used in various industries for their strength, durability, and resistance to UV radiation and chemicals.</p></p>
