<p><p>Fresh Meat Packaging Film is categorized into various types based on the material used. PE (Polyethylene) film is the most common and versatile option, offering good heat resistance and sealing properties. PP (Polypropylene) film is known for its high clarity and moisture resistance. BOPP (Biaxially Oriented Polypropylene) film provides excellent barrier properties. EVOH (Ethylene Vinyl Alcohol) film offers superior oxygen and gas barrier, ensuring longer shelf life. PVC (Polyvinyl Chloride) film is widely used for its good mechanical strength and clarity. PA (Polyamide) film is highly resistant to punctures and abrasions. Other types include films made of various materials like PLA (Polylactic Acid).</p></p>
