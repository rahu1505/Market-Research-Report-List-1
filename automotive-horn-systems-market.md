<p><ul><li>Electronic Horn</li><li>Air Horn</li><li>Electromagnetic Horns</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive horn systems market is divided into three types: electronic horns, air horns, and electromagnetic horns. Electronic horns use an electronically generated sound signal and are commonly found in small cars and motorcycles. Air horns, on the other hand, produce sound by releasing compressed air and are often used in heavy-duty vehicles like trucks and trains. Electromagnetic horns utilize an electromagnet to produce sound and are typically found in commercial vehicles. These different types cater to the specific needs of various vehicles based on their size and purpose.</p></p>
