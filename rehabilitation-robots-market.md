<p><p>Rehabilitation robots are devices designed to assist individuals with disabilities in regaining their physical strength and improving their motor functions. As a result, the market is witnessing a notable increase in research and development activities, leading to the introduction of innovative and advanced robotic systems.</p><p>The current market for rehabilitation robots is also experiencing significant growth. Factors such as a rise in the number of patients requiring rehabilitation, an increase in healthcare expenditure, and advancements in robotic technologies are driving market expansion.
