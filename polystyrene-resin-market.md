<p><p>Polystyrene resin finds its application in various industries, primarily through injection molding, profile extrusion, and sheet extrusion processes. Injection molding involves melting the resin and injecting it into molds to create a wide range of products with complex shapes and intricate details. Profile extrusion utilizes the resin to produce long, continuous shapes like pipes, tubes, and rods. Sheet extrusion, on the other hand, involves creating flat sheets or films of polystyrene resin, commonly used for packaging materials or as protective layers in construction and automotive sectors.</p></p>
