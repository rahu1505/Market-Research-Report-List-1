<p><ul><li>Oil-paper Insulated Cable</li><li>EPDM Insulated Cable</li><li>XLPE Insulated Power Cable</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The water-resistant cables market comprises three types: oil-paper insulated cables, EPDM insulated cables, and XLPE insulated power cables. Oil-paper insulated cables are designed with layers of oil and paper to provide resistance against water ingress. EPDM insulated cables use ethylene propylene diene monomer rubber for enhanced water resistance. XLPE insulated power cables feature cross-linked polyethylene insulation, which offers high resistance to moisture penetration. These cables find application in various industries where water resistance is crucial to ensure reliable and safe electrical connections.</p></p>
