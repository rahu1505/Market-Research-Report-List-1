<p><p>The Anti-Fog Paint Market can be classified into two types - Hydrophilic Anti-Fog and Hydrophobic Anti-Fog. Hydrophilic Anti-Fog paint is designed to attract water molecules and spread them out, preventing the formation of fog. It works by allowing moisture to form a thin film on the surface, thus maintaining clarity. On the other hand, Hydrophobic Anti-Fog paint repels water by creating a barrier on the surface. This prevents moisture from condensing into fog, ensuring clear visibility. Both types aim to mitigate fogging issues in various applications.</p></p>
