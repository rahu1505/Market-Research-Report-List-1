<p><p>CFRP (Carbon Fiber Reinforced Polymer) is used in two different market types: thermosetting and thermoplastic. Thermosetting CFRP is composed of a resin that sets permanently when heated, providing excellent strength and rigidity. Due to its irreversible nature, it cannot be reshaped or recycled. On the other hand, thermoplastic CFRP consists of a resin that softens when heated and solidifies again when cooled. This allows it to be molded and reshaped multiple times, making it more flexible and recyclable. Both types have their unique advantages and applications in industries such as aerospace, automotive, and sporting goods.</p></p>
