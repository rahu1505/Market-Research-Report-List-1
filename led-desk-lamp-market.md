<p><p>The LED desk lamp market can be categorized into two types: wired and wireless. Wired desk lamps require a physical connection to a power source through a cable, offering a stable power supply and uninterrupted lighting. On the other hand, wireless desk lamps operate without the need for direct connection, using rechargeable batteries or induction charging. They provide the flexibility to be used in various locations, without the constraint of a power outlet. Both types of lamps cater to different consumer needs, with wired lamps preferred for consistent power and wireless lamps valued for their portability.</p></p>
