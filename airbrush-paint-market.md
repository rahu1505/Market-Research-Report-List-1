<p><p>Airbrush paint is available in two main types: solvent-based and water-based. Solvent-based airbrush paint uses chemicals like acetone or mineral spirits as its base, which evaporate during the drying process, leaving behind a durable and long-lasting finish. On the other hand, water-based airbrush paint uses water as its base, making it easier to clean up and less toxic compared to solvent-based options. Water-based paint also dries faster, making it more suitable for quick projects. However, solvent-based paint offers better adhesion and is more resistant to moisture and chemicals.</p></p>
