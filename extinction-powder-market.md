<p><p>Extinction powder, also known as extinction pigment or opacifying pigment, is a finely ground substance used to reduce the transparency or adjust the opacity of various materials. Extinction powder particles scatter and absorb light, effectively reducing its transmission and preventing it from reaching the underlying surface.</p><p>The future of the extinction powder market looks promising as the demand for paints, coatings, and inks continues to increase in various industries, including construction, automotive, and packaging.
