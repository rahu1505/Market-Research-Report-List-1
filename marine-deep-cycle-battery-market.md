<p><p>A marine deep-cycle battery is a type of battery designed specifically for use in marine applications such as boats, yachts, and marine vehicles. <p><p>The Marine Deep-Cycle Battery market includes two main types: Flooded Lead Acid Battery and Lithium Ion Battery. Flooded Lead Acid Batteries are traditional and commonly used in marine applications. They have a liquid electrolyte and require regular maintenance. On the other hand, Lithium Ion Batteries are newer and gaining popularity due to their higher energy density, longer lifespan, and lower maintenance requirements. They utilize lithium ions in a solid electrolyte, making them more efficient and lightweight than flooded lead acid batteries.</p></p>
