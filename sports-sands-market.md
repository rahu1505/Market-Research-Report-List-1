<p><p>Sports sands can be classified into four market types based on their particle size - fine sand, medium fine sand, medium sand, and coarse sand. Fine sand consists of fine particles and is ideal for sports like volleyball or golf greens. Medium fine sand is slightly coarser and is used for sports like baseball or softball fields. Medium sand is even coarser and is suitable for sports like soccer or football fields. Coarse sand has the largest particles and is commonly used for sports like beach volleyball or long jump pits.</p></p>
