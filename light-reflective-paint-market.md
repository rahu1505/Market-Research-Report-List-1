<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>Light reflective paint comes in two main types: transparent and opaque. Transparent light reflective paint is designed to be nearly invisible when applied on surfaces such as glass or plastic. It allows light to pass through while still reflecting it, making it suitable for applications where maintaining the appearance of the underlying material is important. On the other hand, opaque light reflective paint completely covers the surface it is applied on, providing a solid reflective layer. This type is commonly used on surfaces like metal or concrete, where visibility and aesthetics are less of a concern.</p></p>
