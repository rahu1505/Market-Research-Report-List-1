<p><p>The Military Ground Robotic Vehicle market consists of two main types: Remote Controlled Vehicles (RCV) and Unmanned Ground Vehicles (UGV). RCVs are controlled by a human operator from a remote location, allowing them to perform tasks such as surveillance, reconnaissance, and explosive ordnance disposal. UGVs, on the other hand, are autonomous vehicles that operate without human intervention. They are equipped with advanced sensors, AI technology, and sophisticated software to perform various military tasks, including reconnaissance, combat support, transport, and logistics. Both RCVs and UGVs play crucial roles in enhancing military effectiveness, reducing human risks, and improving mission success.</p></p>
