<p><p>Next-Gen Firewall market types consist of IPS (Intrusion Prevention System), DPI (Deep Packet Inspection), Application Awareness and Control, and Threat Intelligence Feed. </p><p>IPS analyzes network traffic to identify and block potential threats, preventing malicious activities. DPI examines packet contents to understand the nature of data and detect any malicious content. Application Awareness and Control allow monitoring and control of specific applications within the network. Threat Intelligence Feed provides real-time updates and information about emerging threats, enabling proactive protection against new and evolving cyberattacks. These techniques combined enhance network security by preventing and mitigating various types of cyber threats.</p></p>
