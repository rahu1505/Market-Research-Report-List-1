<p><p>The Ultra High Temperature Furnace Market consists of two types: Horizontal Loading Type and Vertical Loading Type. </p><p>Horizontal Loading Type refers to a furnace configuration where the material is loaded into the furnace horizontally, typically on a conveyor belt or pusher. This type of furnace is suitable for large-scale production and is commonly used for heat treating, sintering, and annealing applications.</p><p>On the other hand, Vertical Loading Type refers to a furnace configuration where the material is loaded into the furnace vertically. This type of furnace is typically used for smaller-scale production and is commonly used for processes like brazing, melting, and laboratory testing.</p></p>
