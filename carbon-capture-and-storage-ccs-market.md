<p><p>Carbon Capture and Storage (CCS) refers to a technology that captures carbon dioxide from industrial sources and permanently stores it underground to mitigate climate change. The three main types of CCS market include Pre-Combustion Carbon Capture, which involves capturing CO2 before fuel combustion; Oxy-Combustion Carbon Capture, which captures CO2 after fuel is combusted using pure oxygen; and Post-Combustion Carbon Capture, which captures CO2 from flue gases after the fuel has been burned. These technologies play a crucial role in reducing greenhouse gas emissions and transitioning to a more sustainable energy sector.</p></p>
