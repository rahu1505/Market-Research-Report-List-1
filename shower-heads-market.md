<p><p>The shower heads market can be classified into two types: fixed showerheads and handheld showerheads. Fixed showerheads are attached to the wall and provide a consistent water stream from a fixed position. They are typically preferred for their simplicity and ease of use. On the other hand, handheld showerheads offer versatility and flexibility as they come with a detachable head that can be held in the hand. This allows users to direct the water stream wherever desired, making them suitable for various purposes such as bathing pets or cleaning the shower.</p></p>
