<p><strong>The Extrusion Coating Grade LDPE Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Tubular Process</li><li>Autoclave Process</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Extrusion Coating Grade LDPE market consists of two main types of processes: Tubular Process and Autoclave Process. In the Tubular Process, LDPE is melted and extruded through a tubular die, resulting in a uniform and continuous film. This process offers better control over film properties and thickness. On the other hand, the Autoclave Process involves the use of a high-pressure reactor to produce LDPE resin, which is then extruded into a film. This process provides flexibility in producing a wide range of LDPE grades with varying properties.</p></p>
