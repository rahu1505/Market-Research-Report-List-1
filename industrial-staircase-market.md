<p><p>The industrial staircase market can be classified into three main types: straight, spiral, and others. Lastly, the "others" category encompasses various staircase designs that do not fit into the straight or spiral classification, such as L-shaped or U-shaped stairs. These different types offer diverse options for industrial settings, each with its own advantages and suitability for specific environments.</p></p>
