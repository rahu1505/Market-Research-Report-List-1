<p><p>Automobile Heat Transfer Equipment market can be categorized into two types: HVAC Thermal Management and Powertrain Thermal Management. HVAC Thermal Management focuses on regulating the temperature inside the vehicle cabin, ensuring passenger comfort, and controlling humidity and air quality. On the other hand, Powertrain Thermal Management is concerned with managing the heat generated by the engine and other components, such as the transmission, exhaust system, and battery. It aims to optimize the performance and efficiency of the powertrain system by dissipating excess heat and maintaining optimal operating temperatures.</p></p>
