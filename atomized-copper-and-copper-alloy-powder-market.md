<p><p>Atomized copper and copper alloy powder refers to fine, spherical particles of copper that are produced through the atomization process, which involves breaking molten copper into small droplets. The market for these powders can be categorized into two types: water atomized and air atomized. In water atomization, molten copper is sprayed with water to rapidly cool and solidify it, producing powder with high purity. On the other hand, air atomization involves using pressurized air to break the molten copper into droplets, resulting in powder with different properties like particle size and compactness.</p></p>
