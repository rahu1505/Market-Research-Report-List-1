<p><p>The emissions control catalyst market is divided into three main types: honeycomb catalyst, plate catalyst, and corrugated catalyst. Honeycomb catalysts are structured like a honeycomb and provide high surface area for catalyst deposition. Plate catalysts consist of flat plates with catalyst coatings and are suitable for smaller applications. Corrugated catalysts are designed with corrugated layers, increasing the surface area for catalytic reactions. These catalyst types are utilized in various industries to reduce emissions of harmful gases and pollutants from automobiles, power plants, and industrial processes.</p></p>
