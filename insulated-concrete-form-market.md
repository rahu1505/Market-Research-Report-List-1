<p><p>The Insulated Concrete Form (ICF) market consists of three main types: Expanded Polystyrene Foam (EPS), Polyurethane Foam, and Others. EPS ICFs are made from expanded polystyrene beads, providing good insulation and cost-effectiveness. Polyurethane Foam ICFs offer higher insulation value and improved strength, making them suitable for extreme weather conditions. The Others category includes alternative materials such as wood fiber and cementitious foam. Each type of ICF provides unique benefits, catering to different construction needs and specifications.</p></p>
