<p><p>The Regenerated Catalyst market can be categorized into two types: Off-site Regeneration and On-site Regeneration. Off-site Regeneration refers to the process where the spent catalyst is transported from the customer's facility to a specialized facility for reactivation. This method saves time and resources for the customer but involves transportation costs. On the other hand, On-site Regeneration involves reactivating the catalyst at the customer's facility itself. It eliminates transportation costs but may require additional infrastructure and equipment. Both methods offer solutions for effective catalyst reuse and optimization of industrial processes.</p></p>
