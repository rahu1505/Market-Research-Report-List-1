<p><p>Two-phase stepper motors are widely used in various industries. They are classified into two market types: bipolar type and unipolar type. Bipolar type stepper motors have two windings per phase and require a bipolar drive circuit. They offer more torque and higher speed capabilities, making them suitable for applications that require high performance. On the other hand, unipolar type stepper motors have center-tapped windings and can operate using a unipolar drive circuit. Although they provide less torque and lower speeds, they are cost-effective and simpler to control, making them popular in less demanding applications.</p></p>
