<p><p>E-commerce, also known as electronic commerce, refers to the buying and selling of goods and services over the internet. Intermediary-oriented e-commerce acts as a middleman between buyers and suppliers. It facilitates transactions, provides value-added services, and ensures smooth communication and collaboration between all parties involved.</p></p>
