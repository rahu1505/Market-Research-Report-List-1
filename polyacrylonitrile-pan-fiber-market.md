<p><p>Polyacrylonitrile (PAN) fiber is a type of synthetic fiber known for its high strength, thermal stability, and chemical resistance. It is commonly used in various industries, including textile, aerospace, and automotive. The PAN fiber market is categorized into two types: wet spinning and dry spinning. Wet spinning involves dissolving PAN polymer in a solvent and extruding it into a coagulating bath. Dry spinning, on the other hand, involves spinning PAN polymer into a dry spinning cell where solvents evaporate, leaving behind solid fibers. Both processes contribute to the production of PAN fibers for different applications.</p></p>
