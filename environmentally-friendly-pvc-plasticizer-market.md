<p><p>The environmentally-friendly PVC plasticizer market includes various types such as adipates esters, trimellitates esters, epoxies esters, and benzoates. Adipates esters are known for their good overall performance and low toxicity. Trimellitates esters offer high-temperature resistance and excellent low-temperature flexibility. Epoxies esters are characterized by their good thermal stability and electrical properties. On the other hand, benzoates are known for their strong solvency and low volatility. Each of these types offers specific benefits and is used for different applications in the PVC plasticizer market.</p></p>
