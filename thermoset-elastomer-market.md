<p><p>Thermoset elastomers are a type of rubber-like material that becomes permanently hardened and inflexible when subjected to high temperatures. These elastomers are produced through a process called vulcanization, where the material is crosslinked to achieve the desired properties of heat resistance, high elasticity, and chemical stability. They find wide applications in various industries such as automotive, electronics, consumer goods, and healthcare, among others.</p><p>The Thermoset Elastomer Market is witnessing significant growth and is expected to continue expanding at a CAGR of 13.8% during the forecast period. The increasing demand for thermoset elastomers can be attributed to their superior properties and advantages over thermoplastic elastomers and other rubber materials. These materials offer excellent heat resistance, durability, electrical insulation, and resistance to oil, grease, and chemicals.</p><p>One of the key drivers of the market is the growing automotive industry. Thermoset elastomers are extensively used in automotive components such as gaskets, seals, O-rings, hoses, and vibration dampers, among others. With the continuous growth in the automotive sector, the demand for thermoset elastomers is expected to surge.</p><p>Moreover, the increasing adoption of thermoset elastomers in the healthcare industry is another factor contributing to market growth. The materials are used in medical device manufacturing, drug delivery systems, and surgical instruments due to their biocompatibility, sterilization resistance, and mechanical properties.</p><p>In terms of regional analysis, the Asia-Pacific region is anticipated to dominate the thermoset elastomer market during the forecast period.
