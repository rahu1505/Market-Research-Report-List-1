<p><p>Water soluble Polyvinyl Alcohol (PVA) films are divided into two main types: electronic grade and industrial grade. </p><p>Electronic grade PVA films are specifically designed for use in electronic devices and circuits. They possess excellent electrical insulation properties and can withstand high temperatures and pressures during the manufacturing process. These films are commonly used as protection layers during the production of electronic components such as printed circuit boards, displays, and semiconductors.</p><p>On the other hand, industrial grade PVA films are utilized in various industrial applications. They provide strong adhesion, good resistance to solvents and chemicals, and excellent mechanical strength. Industrial grade PVA films are commonly used in industries such as packaging, textiles, construction, and agriculture as coatings, adhesives, or as a mold-release agent.</p><p>Overall, the different market types of water soluble PVA films cater to specific needs in the electronic and industrial sectors, respectively.</p></p>
