<p><strong>The FPGA Evaluation Board Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>SRAM</li><li>FLASH</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The FPGA evaluation board market offers two main types: SRAM-based and FLASH-based boards. SRAM-based boards utilize volatile memory that requires continuous power supply to retain data, offering faster reprogramming and debugging capabilities. On the other hand, FLASH-based boards use non-volatile memory that retains data even without power, resulting in greater convenience and reduced power consumption. Both types cater to different customer needs, with SRAM boards favored for rapid prototyping and development, while FLASH boards are preferred for applications requiring data storage and deployment flexibility.</p></p>
