<p><p>Intelligent lighting refers to the use of advanced technology to enhance the functionality and efficiency of lighting systems. There are several types of intelligent lighting markets. Occupancy sensing uses sensors to detect the presence of people and turns on or adjusts lighting based on occupancy levels. Ultrasonic sensors emit sound waves to detect movement and control lighting accordingly.
