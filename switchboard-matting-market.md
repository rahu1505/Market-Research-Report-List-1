<p><ul><li>Type I Switchboard Matting</li><li>Type II Switchboard Matting</li><li>Type III Switchboard Matting</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Switchboard matting refers to a type of insulating material used for the protection and safety of workers dealing with electrical equipment. Type I switchboard matting is made of rubber and is suitable for low voltage applications. Type II matting is also rubber-based but has higher dielectric strength for medium voltage usage. Type III matting is manufactured from a combination of rubber and PVC, designed for high voltage applications. Each type offers varying levels of resistance to electrical current, ensuring worker safety and preventing electrical shock accidents.</p></p>
