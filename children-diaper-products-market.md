<p><p>The children diaper products market can be divided into two main types: cream and emulsion. Emulsions, on the other hand, are liquid-based products that are designed to create a protective barrier on the baby's skin, keeping it hydrated and preventing diaper rash. Both cream and emulsion products serve the purpose of ensuring the comfort and well-being of babies by providing protection against diaper-related issues.</p></p>
