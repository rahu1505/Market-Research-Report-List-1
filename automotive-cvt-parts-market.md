<p><p>The automotive CVT (Continuous Variable Transmission) parts market consists of several types of components. The valve body regulates the flow of fluid in the transmission. Stepper motor controls the ratio of the CVT. Pumps maintain fluid pressure for smooth operation. Planet sets transmit power between gears. Drive belts transfer power from the engine to the transmission. Push belts transmit power in some CVT designs. Lastly, there are other miscellaneous parts that include filters, gaskets, seals, and bearings, which contribute to the overall functioning of the CVT system.</p></p>
