<p><p>Integrated LED downlights are lighting fixtures that combine the light source and housing into a single unit. They come in various types, including surface mount and flush mount. Surface mount downlights are installed directly on the surface, making them highly versatile and suitable for different surfaces such as ceilings or walls. Both options provide efficient and long-lasting lighting solutions for residential or commercial spaces.</p></p>
