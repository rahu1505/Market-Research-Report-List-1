<p><p>ArF Resist refers to a type of resist used in photolithography processes. ArF Dry Resist market involves the use of ArF (argon fluoride) gas for dry lithography, where the resist is exposed using ArF laser alone. On the other hand, ArF Immersion Resist market utilizes liquid immersion lithography, where the resist is immersed in a liquid medium such as water, improving the resolution and enabling greater precision. Both ArF Dry Resist and ArF Immersion Resist cater to different lithography methods, offering distinct advantages and applications in the manufacturing of semiconductors and microchips.</p></p>
