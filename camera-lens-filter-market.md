<p><p>The camera lens filter market encompasses various types of filters for different purposes. Band pass filters allow only a specific range of wavelengths to pass through, while blocking others. Long pass filters transmit longer wavelengths and block shorter ones. Short pass filters are the opposite, transmitting shorter wavelengths while blocking longer ones.
