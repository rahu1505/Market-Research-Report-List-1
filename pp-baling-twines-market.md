<p><p>PP (polypropylene) baling twines are categorized into three market types based on their strength and usage. Light duty twines are designed for lightweight materials and have a lower breaking strength. They are suitable for smaller bales and light-duty applications. Medium duty twines are stronger and used for average-sized bales and general-purpose baling. Heavy duty twines are the most robust, providing high breaking strength for heavy materials and larger bales. They are commonly used for demanding agricultural and industrial applications where maximum strength is required.</p></p>
