<p><p>Detergents are widely used cleaning products that come in various types. Anionic detergents have a negative charge and are effective in removing greasy stains. Cationic detergents have a positive charge and are mainly used for disinfection and fabric softening. Non-ionic detergents do not have any charge and are gentle on surfaces, making them suitable for delicate fabrics. Zwitterionic (Amphoteric) detergents have both positive and negative charges, making them versatile for a range of cleaning tasks. Different detergent types serve specific cleaning purposes and cater to various consumer preferences in the market.</p></p>
