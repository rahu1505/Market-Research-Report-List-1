<p><p>Unsaturated polyester molding compounds are widely used in various industries for their excellent mechanical properties and heat resistance. Sheet molding compounds (SMC) are pre-mixed materials that are compressed and cured to form thin, rigid sheets. They are commonly used for components in electrical insulation, automotive body parts, and construction applications. On the other hand, bulk molding compounds (BMC) are thick, moldable mixtures that can be shaped into complex forms. BMCs are used for manufacturing electrical connectors, automotive components, and household appliances due to their high strength and dimensional stability.</p></p>
