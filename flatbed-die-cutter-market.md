<p><p>The Flatbed Die-Cutter market can be categorized into two main types: Automatic Flatbed Die-Cutter and Semi-automatic Flatbed Die-Cutter. </p><p>Automatic Flatbed Die-Cutters are fully automated machines that can independently perform the entire die-cutting process. They are equipped with advanced technology, including programmable controls and robotic systems, to ensure high precision and efficiency. These machines are suitable for large-scale production and can handle various materials and intricate designs.</p><p>On the other hand, Semi-automatic Flatbed Die-Cutters require some manual intervention during the die-cutting process. Operators load and unload materials manually, while the machine takes care of the cutting process. These machines are ideal for smaller production volumes and offer a more cost-effective alternative to automatic options while still maintaining a decent level of accuracy and productivity.</p></p>
