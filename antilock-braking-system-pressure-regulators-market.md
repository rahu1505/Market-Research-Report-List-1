Integrated ABS systems combine the functions of ABS with other safety features such as electronic stability control and traction control system. This integration reduces the complexity and cost of the system, while also enhancing the overall safety and performance.</p><p>Another significant trend in the market is the development of lightweight ABS pressure regulators.
