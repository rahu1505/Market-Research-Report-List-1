<p><p>The cosmetic pouch film market comprises several types of films used for packaging cosmetics. Aluminum foil is commonly used for its barrier properties against moisture, light, and oxygen. BOPP film (biaxially oriented polypropylene) is known for its clarity, high tensile strength, and resistance to moisture. BOPA film (biaxially oriented polyamide) offers excellent puncture resistance and gas barrier properties. BOPET film (biaxially oriented polyethylene terephthalate) is a popular choice due to its thermal stability and dimensional strength. CPP film (cast polypropylene) provides excellent clarity, moisture resistance, and heat sealability. LLDPE film (linear low-density polyethylene) offers high flexibility and impact resistance. Lastly, the "Other" category refers to various films like PVC, LDPE, and HDPE that may have specific properties suitable for cosmetic packaging.</p></p>
