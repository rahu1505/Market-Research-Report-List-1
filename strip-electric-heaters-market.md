<p><p>Strip electric heaters are heating elements that consist of a strip of resistive material, typically nichrome or stainless steel, which generates heat when an electric current passes through it. They offer benefits such as high efficiency, uniform heating, precise temperature control, and ease of installation.</p><p>The current outlook of the strip electric heaters market is positive due to their increasing demand across various industries.
