Columns and beams provide structural support, while floors and roofs act as horizontal surfaces. Girders are large beams that support other structural members. Walls provide enclosure and separation. Staircases enable vertical movement. Lintels are horizontal beams that support the load above openings. Paving slabs are used for exterior flooring purposes. These products offer cost-effective and efficient construction solutions.</p></p>
