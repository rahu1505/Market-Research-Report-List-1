<p><p>MOCVD (Metal Organic Chemical Vapor Deposition) susceptors are a critical component in the manufacturing process of semiconductor devices. They act as a substrate for the growth of crystalline films during the MOCVD process. The primary function of the susceptor is to provide good thermal conductivity, mechanical strength, and chemical resistance, while maintaining uniform temperature distribution throughout the wafer. MOCVD susceptors are generally made of materials like graphite, silicon carbide, or molybdenum.</p><p>The MOCVD Susceptors Market is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period. The increasing demand for power electronics, optoelectronics, and compound semiconductor devices is driving the market growth. <p><p>MOCVD (metal-organic chemical vapor deposition) susceptors are used in the semiconductor industry for the growth of thin films on substrates. There are two main types of MOCVD susceptors in the market: CVD SiC coated graphite and CVI SiC coated graphite. CVD SiC has a chemical vapor deposition SiC coating, while CVI SiC has a chemical vapor infiltration SiC coating.
