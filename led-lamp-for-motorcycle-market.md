<p><p>The LED lamp market for motorcycles can be categorized into three types: headlamps, taillights, and stoplights. Headlamps are used to illuminate the road ahead and ensure visibility for the rider during low-light conditions. Taillights are positioned at the rear of the motorcycle and serve to indicate the presence and position of the bike to other road users. Stoplights are activated when the rider applies the brakes, alerting those behind about the motorcycle's intention to stop or slow down. These different types of LED lamps play crucial roles in enhancing safety on the road for motorcycle riders.</p></p>
