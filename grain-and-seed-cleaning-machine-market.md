<p><p>The Grain and Seed Cleaning Machine market can be categorized into two types: Pre-Cleaning Type and Fine Cleaning Type. Pre-Cleaning machines are used to remove larger impurities like straw, stones, and clods from the grains and seeds. They are generally used in the initial stages of processing. On the other hand, Fine Cleaning machines are designed to remove smaller impurities like dust, small stones, and broken grains. They are used to ensure a higher level of cleanliness and purity in the final product.</p></p>
