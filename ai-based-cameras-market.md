<p><p>AI-based cameras are divided into two main market types: cloud-based and on-premises. In the cloud-based market, cameras are connected to a cloud platform where artificial intelligence algorithms are run. This enables real-time analysis of video footage, efficient storage, and easy access to data from any location. On the other hand, in the on-premises market, the cameras and AI algorithms are located locally, allowing for greater control over data privacy and faster response times. This market type is suitable for applications where data security is a top priority or where low latency is crucial.</p></p>
