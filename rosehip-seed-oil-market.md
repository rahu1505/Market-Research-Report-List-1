<p><p>Rosehip seed oil is available in two main types: essential oil and compound oil. Essential oil is obtained through a process of distillation, extracting volatile compounds from the rosehip seeds. It contains pure and concentrated properties that offer numerous benefits for skincare and aromatherapy. On the other hand, compound oil is a blend of essential oil with a carrier oil, often diluted to a certain extent. Compound oil is more commonly used for topical application, as it provides a milder and ready-to-use solution to reap the benefits of rosehip seed oil.</p></p>
