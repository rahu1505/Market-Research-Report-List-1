<p><p>The Piston Plastering Machine market includes two types: Single Piston and Double Piston machines. In Single Piston machines, there is only one piston that operates to pump and spray the plastering material onto the desired surface. On the other hand, Double Piston machines consist of two pistons working simultaneously, which allows for a higher output and faster plastering process. The choice between the two types depends on the specific requirements of the project, such as the surface area to be covered and the desired speed of plastering.</p></p>
