<p><p>The Metallurgical Coal market is categorized into different types based on their properties and usage. Hard Coking Coals (HCC) are high-quality coals used in steelmaking, while Medium Coking Coal falls between HCC and Semi-soft Coking Coal (SSCC) in terms of quality. SSCC has a lower coking ability and is often blended with HCC to produce coke for steel production. Pulverized Coal Injection (PCI) Coal is used as a supplementary fuel in blast furnaces to enhance the efficiency of steelmaking. Each market type serves a specific purpose in the metallurgical coal industry.</p></p>
