<p><p>The Ceramic Ultrafiltration Membrane Market comprises two main types: Flat-sheet Membrane and Pipe Membrane. Flat-sheet Membrane refers to a filtration system where thin, porous sheets made of ceramic material are used to remove impurities from liquids or gases. These membranes are typically used in water treatment processes. On the other hand, Pipe Membrane involves the use of hollow ceramic tubes to filter substances, often used in industries like food and beverage, pharmaceuticals, and chemical processing. Both types offer efficient and reliable filtration capabilities for various applications.</p></p>
