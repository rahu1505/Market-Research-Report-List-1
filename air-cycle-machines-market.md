<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>Air Cycle Machines (ACMs) are commonly used in the aviation industry for air conditioning and heating purposes. There are several types of ACMs available in the market. Simple cycle ACMs utilize a single compressor and turbine wheel to power the system. Two-wheel bootstrap ACMs use two turbines that are powered by separate compressors, increasing efficiency. Three-wheel ACMs consist of three turbines and compressors, further improving efficiency. Four-wheel/Dual-spool ACMs utilize two spools, each with its own compressor and turbine, offering enhanced performance and fuel efficiency.</p></p>
