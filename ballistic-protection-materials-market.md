<p><p>Ballistic protection materials refer to materials used to protect individuals or objects from projectiles, such as bullets or fragments. There are three main types of these materials: Poly-Ethylene (PE) based, Aramid (Aromatic Amide) based, and Ceramic Fabric. </p><p>PE-based materials are lightweight and have high energy absorption capacity. Aramid-based materials, like Kevlar, offer excellent resistance to impact and heat while being lightweight. Ceramic fabric, often combined with other materials, provides superior resistance against armor-piercing projectiles due to its high hardness. These materials play a crucial role in manufacturing bulletproof vests, helmets, vehicle armor, and other protective equipment.</p></p>
