<p><p>Clear coatings are protective finishes applied to surfaces to enhance their appearance and offer resistance against corrosion, UV rays, and wear. There are several types of clear coatings available in the market, including solvent-borne, water-borne, and powder coating. Solvent-borne coatings are made by dissolving resins in organic solvents, while water-borne coatings use water as the primary medium. Powder coating involves applying a dry powder electrostatically and then curing it with heat. Apart from these three major types, there are other clear coating options available, each with its unique composition, application process, and performance characteristics.</p></p>
