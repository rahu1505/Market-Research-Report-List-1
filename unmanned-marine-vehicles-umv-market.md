<p><p>Unmanned Marine Vehicles (UMVs) are autonomous or remotely controlled vessels that are capable of operating underwater or on the surface without a human crew. First, the increasing need for maritime surveillance and security is driving the demand for UMVs, as they can operate in dangerous or remote areas without risking human lives. These technological advancements are expected to drive the growth of the UMV market in the coming years.</p><p>Moreover, the growing demand for underwater exploration and research activities, particularly in sectors like oil and gas, oceanography, and marine biology, is also contributing to the market's growth.
