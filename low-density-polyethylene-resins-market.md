<p><p>Low Density Polyethylene (LDPE) Resins Market Types include Type I and Type II. Type I is characterized by resins that have high clarity and good stiffness. These resins are commonly used in applications such as packaging films, grocery bags, and shrink films. Type II, on the other hand, refers to LDPE resins with lower clarity and increased flexibility. This type is often used in applications like plastic wraps, garbage bags, and agricultural films. Both Type I and Type II LDPE resins serve different purposes within the market based on their specific properties and applications.</p></p>
