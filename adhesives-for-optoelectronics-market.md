<p><p>The adhesives for optoelectronics market can be categorized into three types: liquid, solid, and paste adhesives. Liquid adhesives are in a fluid state and are commonly used for bonding delicate optical components due to their ability to fill microscopic gaps. Solid adhesives, on the other hand, are pre-formed into specific shapes and require heating or UV light to reach their bonding state. They are ideal for applications that require high strength and stability. Paste adhesives have a thick consistency and are often used for bonding surfaces that need to be repositioned before curing.</p></p>
