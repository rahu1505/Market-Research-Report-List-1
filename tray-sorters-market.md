<p><p>Tray sorters are used in various industries for efficient sorting and distribution of items. Push tray sorters are designed to transport trays in a straight line, pushing them forward for sorting. Tilt tray sorters use tilting mechanisms to divert trays into different chutes for sorting. Split tray sorters have partitions that divide a single tray into multiple sections for simultaneous sorting. Other types of tray sorters include pop-up sorters and bomb-bay sorters, which utilize unique mechanisms for efficient sorting. These various types cater to different sorting needs and contribute to the overall efficiency of material handling operations.</p></p>
