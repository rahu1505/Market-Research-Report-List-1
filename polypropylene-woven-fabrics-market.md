<p><p>Polypropylene woven fabrics are widely used in various industries for their excellent strength, durability, and versatility. The market for these fabrics can be categorized into two types: laminated and unlaminated. Laminated polypropylene fabrics have an additional layer of protective coating, enhancing their water and chemical resistance. They are ideal for applications requiring a higher level of protection, such as geotextiles or packaging materials. On the other hand, unlaminated polypropylene fabrics lack the protective coating but still offer adequate strength and are commonly used in applications like bags, agriculture, and furniture upholstery.</p></p>
