<p><p>The Automatic Train Operation Systems market consists of three main types: Semi-automatic Train Operation, Driverless Train Operation, and Unattended Train Operation. Semi-automatic Train Operation involves the train being operated by a driver who has partial control over the train's functions, with automated assistance. Driverless Train Operation, as the name suggests, eliminates the need for a driver as the train is operated solely by automated systems. Unattended Train Operation takes automation a step further, with no on-board staff required, and the train running entirely on its own.</p></p>
