<p><p>The Fluorescent Magnetic Particles Flaw Detector market comprises two main types: Magnetic Yokes and Magnetic Benches. Magnetic Yokes are handheld devices that generate a magnetic field when an electric current passes through them. They are portable and easy to use, making them ideal for on-site inspections. Magnetic Benches, on the other hand, are stationary machines that generate a consistent magnetic field. They are more suitable for larger, complex components or high-volume testing. Both types are used in non-destructive testing to detect flaws, cracks, or defects in metal structures by highlighting them with fluorescent magnetic particles.</p></p>
