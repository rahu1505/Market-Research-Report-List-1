<p><p>Venturi desuperheaters are devices used in steam systems to reduce the temperature of superheated steam. There are two main types of Venturi desuperheaters: single Venturi desuperheaters and double Venturi desuperheaters. </p><p>Single Venturi desuperheaters have a single Venturi section where the cooling water is injected into the steam flow. This type is commonly used in smaller systems where a moderate level of desuperheating is required.</p><p>Double Venturi desuperheaters have two Venturi sections, providing a greater degree of cooling and desuperheating. This type is typically used in larger industrial applications where a high level of desuperheating is necessary.</p></p>
