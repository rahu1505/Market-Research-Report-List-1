<p><p>LDPE Geomembrane is a type of liner used to prevent liquid seepage in various applications. There are three main types of LDPE Geomembrane production methods: extrusion, calendering, and blown film. Extrusion involves melting and forcing the LDPE material through a die, creating a continuous sheet. Calendering involves sandwiching the LDPE material between heated rollers to form a flat sheet. Blown film involves blowing LDPE material into a tubular shape, which is then flattened to create a durable sheet. These different production methods offer various performance characteristics, including flexibility, durability, and cost-effectiveness, to meet specific project requirements.</p></p>
