<p><p>The market for house wraps consists of various types such as asphalt-impregnated paper or fiberglass, micro-perforated, spunbonded nonwoven, woven, and drainable house wraps. Asphalt-impregnated paper or fiberglass wraps provide excellent moisture resistance and are widely used in construction. Micro-perforated wraps offer breathability and prevent moisture buildup. Spunbonded nonwoven wraps are lightweight and easy to install. Woven wraps are durable and provide added strength. Drainable house wraps have improved drainage capabilities to redirect water away from a building. These different types cater to various construction needs and preferences.</p></p>
