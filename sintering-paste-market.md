<p><strong>The Sintering Paste Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pressure Sintering</li><li>Pressure-less Sintering</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Sintering paste is categorized into types known as pressure sintering and pressure-less sintering. Pressure sintering involves subjecting the paste to high external pressure during the sintering process, allowing for better bonding and densification of the materials. On the other hand, pressure-less sintering is carried out without external pressure, relying on factors such as temperature and time for sintering. Both methods have their advantages and applications, with pressure sintering being ideal for high-performance materials and pressure-less sintering providing cost-effective options for certain applications.</p></p>
