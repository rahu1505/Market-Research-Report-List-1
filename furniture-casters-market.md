<p><p>The Furniture Casters Market consists of two main types: Swivel Casters and Rigid Casters. Swivel Casters are designed to rotate 360 degrees, allowing for easy maneuverability and change in direction. They are commonly used on furniture items that require frequent movement and navigation. On the other hand, Rigid Casters do not rotate and are fixed in one direction, providing stability and straight-line movement. These are typically found on furniture that doesn't require frequent shifting, offering a more secure and immobile option.</p></p>
