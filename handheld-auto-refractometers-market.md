<p><p>Handheld auto refractometers are portable devices used for quick and accurate measurement of refractive index and other parameters. Electric-powered auto refractometers are battery-operated and employ an electric motor to rotate the prism and display results electronically. Gas-powered auto refractometers, on the other hand, use gas pressure to rotate the prism and measure the refractive index. Both types are widely used in various industries, including medicine, research, and food processing, due to their convenience, portability, and ability to provide precise measurements in a handheld format.</p></p>
