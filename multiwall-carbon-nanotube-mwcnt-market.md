<p><ul><li>Two Decks</li><li>More than Two Decks</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Multiwall carbon nanotubes (MWCNT) are categorized into two types based on the number of walls they possess: two decks and more than two decks. Two decks MWCNTs consist of two concentric walls, offering enhanced electrical conductivity and mechanical strength compared to single-wall carbon nanotubes. On the other hand, more than two decks MWCNTs have three or more concentric walls, further enhancing their properties and making them suitable for various applications such as energy storage, electronics, and aerospace. These distinct types enable the selection of MWCNTs based on specific requirements and uses.</p></p>
