<p><p>Terrazzo is a type of flooring material that consists of chips of various materials embedded in a binder. There are two main types of terrazzo markets: inorganic terrazzo and epoxy terrazzo. Inorganic terrazzo is made with a cement binder and includes materials such as marble, granite, and quartz. It is widely used in high-traffic areas due to its durability and resistance to stains and abrasions. On the other hand, epoxy terrazzo is made with an epoxy resin binder, providing a seamless and highly customizable flooring option. It is known for its versatility and aesthetic appeal, making it ideal for creative designs and intricate patterns.</p></p>
