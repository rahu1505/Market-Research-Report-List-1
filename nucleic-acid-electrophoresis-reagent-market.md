<p><strong>The Nucleic Acid Electrophoresis Reagent Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gel Electrophoresis</li><li>Capillary Electrophoresis</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The nucleic acid electrophoresis reagent market consists of two types: gel electrophoresis and capillary electrophoresis. Gel electrophoresis involves the separation of nucleic acids using a gel matrix, while capillary electrophoresis utilizes narrow capillaries for the separation process. Gel electrophoresis is known for its versatility and compatibility with various sample sizes, while capillary electrophoresis offers high resolution and sensitivity due to the small size of the capillaries employed. Both techniques play a vital role in the analysis and characterization of nucleic acids in various research and diagnostic applications.</p></p>
