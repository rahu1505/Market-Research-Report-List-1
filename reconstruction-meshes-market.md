<p><p>Reconstruction meshes, also known as surgical meshes or hernia meshes, are medical devices made from synthetic materials that are used to provide support and strengthen weakened or damaged tissues in surgical procedures. <p><p>The reconstruction meshes market consists of several types, including metal, plastic, biological materials, and other materials. Plastic meshes, typically made of polypropylene, offer flexibility and are often used in hernia repair. Biological meshes are derived from human or animal tissue and are suitable for cases requiring natural tissue regeneration.
