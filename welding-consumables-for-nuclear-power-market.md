<p><p>Welding consumables for the nuclear power market include various types such as Shielded Metal Arc Welding (SMAW), Submerged Arc Welding (SAW), and others. SMAW involves using a flux-coated electrode, which creates a protective shield for the weld area. SAW employs a granular flux that creates a blanket over the weld, reducing atmospheric contamination. Other types of welding consumables may include Gas Tungsten Arc Welding (GTAW) or Gas Metal Arc Welding (GMAW), each offering unique benefits and applications in the nuclear power sector.</p></p>
