<p><p>The Vegetable Parchment is categorized into two main market types: Plain Vegetable Parchment and Siliconized Genuine Vegetable Parchment. Plain Vegetable Parchment refers to a natural, uncoated parchment paper made from cellulose fibers. It is used in various applications like food packaging, baking, and cooking due to its excellent heat resistance and non-stick properties. On the other hand, Siliconized Genuine Vegetable Parchment is a plain parchment paper coated with a thin layer of silicone. It provides additional benefits such as enhanced non-stick properties and increased resistance to grease, making it ideal for baking and food wrapping purposes.</p></p>
