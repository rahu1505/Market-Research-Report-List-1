<p><p>Fluorosilicone Elastomer (FVMQ) is a type of synthetic rubber that combines the properties of fluorocarbon and silicone elastomers. The FVMQ market is divided into three types: liquid FVMQ, solid FVMQ, and semisolid FVMQ. </p><p>Liquid FVMQ is a low viscosity product used for applications such as molding and casting. Solid FVMQ, on the other hand, has high mechanical strength and is typically used for seals, gaskets, and O-rings. Semisolid FVMQ falls between the liquid and solid forms and is commonly employed in lubricants and grease formulations. Each type serves specific purposes within various industries that rely on FVMQ elastomers.</p></p>
