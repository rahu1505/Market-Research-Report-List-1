<p><p>The ordinary rectifier diode market consists of two types: single tube and double tube. Single tube rectifiers have only one diode element and are commonly used in low voltage applications. They allow current to flow in only one direction, converting AC to DC. In contrast, double tube rectifiers have two diode elements connected in a bridge configuration, allowing current to flow in both directions. They are used in high voltage applications and can convert both AC and DC power efficiently.</p></p>
