<p><p>The parking ticket machine market can be broadly categorized into two types: parking meters (single space) and parking kiosks (multi-space). Parking meters are standalone devices that are installed on individual parking spots, allowing users to pay for the time they occupy that space. On the other hand, parking kiosks are centralized devices that provide multiple parking spaces within a designated area, allowing users to pay for their parking session using a single machine. Both types aim to streamline parking management and revenue collection processes for municipalities and parking operators.</p></p>
