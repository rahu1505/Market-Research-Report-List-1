<p><ul><li>Single Component</li><li>Double Component</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive silicone adhesives and sealants are commonly available in two types: single component and double component. Single component adhesives and sealants come ready-to-use and do not require any mixing or curing agents prior to application. They offer simplicity and ease of use, but their curing time may be longer. On the other hand, double component adhesives and sealants require the mixing of two components, typically a base and a curing agent, before application. They offer shorter curing times and enhanced bonding strength, making them ideal for critical automotive sealing applications.</p></p>
