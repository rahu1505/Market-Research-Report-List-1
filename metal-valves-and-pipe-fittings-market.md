<p><p>The metal valves and pipe fittings market can be categorized into four types based on operation methods: manual, pneumatic, hydraulic, and electric. Manual valves and fittings are operated manually, turning a handle or lever. Pneumatic valves and fittings use compressed air to adjust flow and pressure. Hydraulic valves and fittings rely on hydraulic fluids to control flow and pressure. Electric valves and fittings are operated using electric power, providing precise control and automation. Each type offers unique features and benefits, catering to different industrial applications and requirements.</p></p>
