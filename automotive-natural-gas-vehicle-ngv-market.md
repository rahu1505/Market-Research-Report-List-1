<p><p>The Automotive Natural Gas Vehicle (NGV) market encompasses two main types: Compressed Natural Gas (CNG) vehicles and Liquefied Natural Gas (LNG) vehicles. CNG vehicles store natural gas at high pressure, typically in cylindrical tanks, and are suitable for various vehicle types. On the other hand, LNG vehicles store natural gas in a liquefied form, which requires cryogenic tanks that maintain extremely low temperatures. Both CNG and LNG vehicles offer lower emissions and reduced fuel costs compared to traditional gasoline or diesel vehicles, promoting the use of a cleaner and more sustainable fuel source.</p></p>
