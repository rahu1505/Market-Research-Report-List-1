C4 hydrocarbons refer to a mixture of hydrocarbons containing four carbon atoms, while dehydrogenation of n-butane involves the removal of hydrogen from n-butane to obtain butadiene. Ethanol and butenes are also used as feedstocks for butadiene production. Each of these market types offers different methods and processes for extracting butadiene.</p></p>
