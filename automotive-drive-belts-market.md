<p><strong>The Automotive Drive Belts Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Timing Belt</li><li>Serpentine Belt</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive drive belts are crucial components in vehicles, responsible for transferring power from the engine to various systems. Timing belts control the timing of the engine's valves, ensuring proper combustion. They are made of durable materials like rubber with tooth-shaped ridges for improved grip. Serpentine belts, on the other hand, connect various engine components, such as the alternator and water pump. They are flat and smooth, often made of synthetic rubber. Both belts play significant roles in ensuring the smooth operation of vehicles, but differ in functionality and design.</p></p>
