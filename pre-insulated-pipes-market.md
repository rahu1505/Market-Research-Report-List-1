<p><p>Pre-insulated pipes are categorized into two market types: below ground pipes and above ground pipes. Below ground pipes are installed in the ground and are commonly used for district heating and cooling systems, as well as oil and gas pipelines. Above ground pipes, on the other hand, are installed above the ground and are mainly used for applications such as thermal energy distribution and chilled water systems. Both types of pre-insulated pipes are essential for efficient and reliable transportation of fluids in various industrial and commercial sectors.</p></p>
