<p><ul><li>Ground-based Broadband</li><li>Satellite Broadband</li></ul></p>
<p><p>In the in-flight internet market, there are two main types: ground-based broadband and satellite broadband. Ground-based broadband relies on a network of cell towers on the ground to provide internet connectivity to aircraft. This type offers high-speed internet but is limited to flight paths that are within range of ground networks. On the other hand, satellite broadband uses a network of satellites to provide internet connectivity, enabling coverage even in remote areas. While it offers global coverage, satellite broadband typically has lower bandwidth and higher latency compared to ground-based broadband.</p></p>
