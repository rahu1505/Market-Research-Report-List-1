<p><p>The Yarn Fiber Lubricant market consists of different types, namely DTY, FDY, POY, and Other market. DTY stands for Drawn Textured Yarn, which is a textured yarn made by drawing, twisting, and setting the filament fibers. FDY refers to Fully Drawn Yarn, a continuous filament yarn produced through the process of drawing and texturing. POY stands for Partially Oriented Yarn, which is a filament yarn with partially oriented molecular chains. Lastly, the Other market comprises various types of yarn fiber lubricants that do not fall into the categories of DTY, FDY, or POY.</p></p>
