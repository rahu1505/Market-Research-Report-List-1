<p><ul><li>Casting</li><li>Forging</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The market for SUV steel wheels can be divided into three main types: casting, forging, and others. Casting involves pouring molten steel into a mold to obtain the desired wheel shape. This method provides high production rates and complex designs. Forging, on the other hand, involves shaping the steel by applying extreme pressure and heat. This process results in stronger and more durable wheels suitable for heavy-duty applications. Lastly, the "other" market includes alternative methods like rim rolling and plate punching that offer unique wheel characteristics.</p></p>
