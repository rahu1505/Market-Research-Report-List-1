<p><p>Nano silver wire conductive film market types include single-sided structure and double-sided structure. The single-sided structure refers to a film where silver wires are applied on only one side of the substrate, providing conductivity to that side only. On the other hand, the double-sided structure means that silver wires are applied on both sides of the substrate, allowing for conduction on both sides. These different structures cater to varying needs and applications in industries such as electronics, solar panels, and touch screens.</p></p>
