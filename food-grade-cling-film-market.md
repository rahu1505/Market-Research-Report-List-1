PE film is widely used as it is cost-effective and has good clarity and flexibility. PVC film offers good barrier properties and is resistant to grease and oil. PVDC film provides excellent oxygen and moisture barrier, making it suitable for perishable food items. PMP film is heat-resistant and has good clarity. Other types refer to films made from different materials like biodegradable or compostable films.</p></p>
