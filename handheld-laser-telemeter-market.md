<p><p>Handheld Laser Telemeter is a device used for measuring distances using laser technology. The market for these devices can be categorized into two types: Phase Method Telemeter and Pulse Method Telemeter. </p><p>Phase Method Telemeter utilizes the phase difference of the laser beam to measure the distance. It provides accurate measurements with high resolution, making it suitable for precise applications like surveying or mapping.</p><p>On the other hand, Pulse Method Telemeter determines distance by measuring the time it takes for the laser pulse to travel and return. It is commonly used in scenarios where high speed and long-range measurements are required, such as sports, military operations, or construction.</p></p>
