<p><p>The automotive batteries market can be divided into various types, including VRLA (valve-regulated lead-acid) battery, flooded battery, and others. VRLA battery refers to a maintenance-free battery that uses a combination of gel and absorbent glass mat technology. Flooded batteries are conventional lead-acid batteries that contain liquid sulfuric acid electrolyte. The "others" category encompasses alternative technologies such as lithium-ion batteries and nickel-metal hydride batteries. These batteries play a crucial role in automotive applications, providing the necessary power for starting the vehicle's engine, as well as supporting various electrical systems during operation.</p></p>
