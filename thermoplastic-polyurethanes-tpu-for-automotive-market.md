<p><p>Thermoplastic Polyurethanes (TPU) are versatile materials widely used in the automotive market due to their excellent properties such as durability, flexibility, and resistance to abrasion and chemicals. There are different types of TPU based on their chemical compositions. Polyester-based TPU offers high mechanical strength and dimensional stability, making it suitable for applications that require tough and rigid materials. Polyether-based TPU, on the other hand, exhibits superior elasticity, making it ideal for flexible components. Polycaprolactone-based TPU combines the benefits of both polyester and polyether TPUs, offering a balance of strength and elasticity for various automotive applications.</p></p>
