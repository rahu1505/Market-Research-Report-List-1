<p><p>EFEM stands for Equipment Front-End Module and it refers to the mechanical interface between the wafer handling equipment and the fabrication tool in a semiconductor manufacturing facility. It acts as a bridge between the material handling systems and the processing tools, ensuring the smooth transfer of wafers during the production process. EFEM is an integral part of the semiconductor manufacturing process and enables the automation and optimization of production flow, improving efficiency and reducing costs.</p><p>Sorters, on the other hand, are devices used to identify and separate objects based on specific characteristics. In the context of the semiconductor industry, sorters are used to inspect and sort wafers based on their quality, size, and other parameters. They are crucial in ensuring that only the highest quality wafers are passed on to the subsequent processing steps.</p><p>In recent years, the EFEM & Sorters market has witnessed significant growth due to the increasing demand for semiconductors in various sectors such as consumer electronics, automotive, and healthcare. The growing adoption of automation and robotics in semiconductor manufacturing facilities has also driven the market growth.</p><p>One of the key trends in the EFEM & Sorters market is the integration of advanced technologies such as artificial intelligence (AI) and machine learning (ML) to enhance the efficiency and accuracy of the sorting process.
