<p><p>Mold discharging agents are chemical substances used to aid in the release of molds from various materials, preventing them from sticking or adhering to the mold surface. There are two types of mold discharging agents: internal release agents and external release agents. Internal release agents are added directly to the material being molded, improving the flow and release properties of the material. On the other hand, external release agents are applied on the surface of the mold, creating a barrier between the mold and the material to enhance the separation process.</p></p>
