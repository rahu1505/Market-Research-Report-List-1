<p><p>Pipe-Tank Coatings refer to protective coatings applied on pipes and tanks used in various industries. There are two main types of coatings available in the market - Powder and Liquid coatings. Powder coatings are a dry, solvent-free form that is sprayed onto the surface and then heat-cured to form a protective layer. Liquid coatings, on the other hand, are applied as a liquid and then dry or cure over time, forming a protective film. Both types offer different advantages and are chosen based on specific requirements for durability, application ease, and environmental considerations.</p></p>
