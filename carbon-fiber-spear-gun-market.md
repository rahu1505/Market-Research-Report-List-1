<p><p>The market for carbon fiber spear guns comprises two main types: pneumatic guns and sling guns. Pneumatic guns utilize compressed air as the main propelling force, which is stored in a reservoir until triggered by pulling the trigger mechanism. These guns are known for their accuracy and power. On the other hand, sling guns rely on a rubber band or bungee-type sling to shoot the spear forward. Sling guns are favored by spearfishing enthusiasts for their simplicity and quiet operation. Both types of guns offer unique advantages and cater to different preferences within the spearfishing community.</p></p>
