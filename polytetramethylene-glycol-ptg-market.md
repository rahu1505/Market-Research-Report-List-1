<p><p>Polytetramethylene glycol (PTG), also known as polytetrahydrofuran (PTMO), is a synthetic polymer that belongs to the class of polyether polyols. PTG offers excellent chemical resistance, low temperature flexibility, and high resilience, making it a preferred choice in various applications.</p><p>The market analysis of polytetramethylene glycol suggests a positive growth trajectory. Factors such as increasing demand for polyurethane elastomers and rising investments in the automotive sector are driving the growth of the market. The versatile nature of PTG allows it to be used in various textile applications, such as in the production of synthetic fibers, woven fabrics, and non-woven textiles.</p><p>Furthermore, the construction industry is also contributing to the market growth as PTG finds applications in coatings, adhesives, and sealants used in construction materials like paints, varnishes, and concrete adhesives.</p><p>The latest trend observed in the PTG market is the increasing demand for bio-based PTG.
