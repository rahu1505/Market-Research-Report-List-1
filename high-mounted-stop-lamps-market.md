<p><p>The high-mounted stop lamp market comprises three main types: LED High Level Brake Lamp, LED Centre High Mounted Stop Lamp, and Centre High Mounted Stop Lamp. LED High Level Brake Lamp is a rear brake light positioned at the top of the vehicle, often using LED technology. LED Centre High Mounted Stop Lamp is a similar light, but it is positioned in the center of the high-mounted location. Centre High Mounted Stop Lamp refers to a traditional brake light located at the top center of the vehicle's rear end. These variations serve the same purpose of indicating braking to the vehicles behind.</p></p>
