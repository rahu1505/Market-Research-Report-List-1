<p><p>The Cesium Iodide market consists of three major types: CsI (Tl), CsI (Na), and CsI Pure. CsI (Na) indicates Cesium Iodide doped with Sodium, commonly used in photodiodes and scintillation detectors. Each variant finds specific applications and demand in diverse industries such as medical imaging, security, and scientific research.</p></p>
