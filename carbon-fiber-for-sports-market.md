<p><p>The carbon fiber for sports market can be categorized into three main types: PAN-Based Carbon Fiber, Viscose-Based Carbon Fiber, and Pitch-Based Carbon Fiber. PAN-Based Carbon Fiber is made from polyacrylonitrile and is widely used due to its high tensile strength and superior mechanical properties. Viscose-Based Carbon Fiber is derived from cellulose and offers good heat resistance and electrical conductivity. Pitch-Based Carbon Fiber is produced from petroleum or coal tar pitch and is known for its exceptional stiffness and low thermal expansion. These different types cater to various sports applications, providing specific qualities required for optimal performance.</p></p>
