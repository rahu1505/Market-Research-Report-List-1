<p><p>An All-Iron Redox Flow Battery (AIRFB) is a type of energy storage system that uses two liquid electrolytes containing iron ions as the active materials. It operates by circulating the electrolytes through separate compartments, where the iron ions undergo redox reactions, converting chemical energy into electrical energy. AIRFBs are known for their high energy density, long cycle life, and scalability, making them suitable for large-scale applications such as renewable energy integration and grid stabilization.</p><p>In terms of market analysis, the All-Iron Redox Flow Battery Market is expected to witness significant growth in the coming years.
