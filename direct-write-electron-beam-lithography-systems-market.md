<p><p>Direct-write electron beam lithography (EBL) systems are advanced devices used in nanofabrication processes. There are two main types of EBL systems: Gaussian beam EBL and shaped beam EBL. Gaussian beam EBL systems utilize a uniform and symmetrical beam for lithography, enabling accurate patterning of features with precise dimensions. On the other hand, shaped beam EBL systems use tailored electron beam shapes to achieve more complex and intricate patterning. Both systems are integral to research and development in nanotechnology, allowing for the fabrication of nanostructures with high resolution and precision.</p></p>
