<p><p>Elemental fertilizers are commonly classified into three types: nitrogen, phosphate, and potash fertilizers. Nitrogen fertilizers provide essential nutrients for plant growth, promoting greener leaves and increased protein production. Phosphate fertilizers supply plants with phosphorous, supporting root development, blooming, and fruiting. Potash fertilizers provide potassium, which aids in disease resistance, water retention, and overall plant vigor. These types of fertilizers are essential for maintaining healthy plant growth and increasing crop yields in agriculture.</p></p>
