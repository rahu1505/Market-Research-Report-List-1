<p><p>Nanoimprint lithography (NIL) is a highly precise patterning technique used in nanofabrication. NIL consumables are materials necessary for the imprinting process. Nanoimprint resins are liquid materials that are patterned and solidified on a substrate during imprinting. They provide the required mechanical and optical properties for imprinting. Nanoimprint resists are photosensitive materials that undergo a chemical reaction upon exposure to light, enabling patterning and transferring the pattern onto the substrate. Templates, on the other hand, are patterned structures used to imprint the desired pattern onto the substrate. These consumables are crucial components for successful nanoimprint lithography.</p></p>
