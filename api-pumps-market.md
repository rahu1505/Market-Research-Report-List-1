<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The API Pumps Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Type I</li><li>Type II</li></ul></p>
<p><p>API (American Petroleum Institute) Pumps are classified into two types: Type I and Type II. Type I pumps are usually single stage, horizontal, end-suction centrifugal pumps with a back pull-out design. These pumps are commonly used for petroleum and petrochemical industry applications, providing high flow rates at low to medium pressures. On the other hand, Type II pumps are typically multistage, horizontal, and segmented ring pumps. They offer higher pressure capabilities, making them suitable for various industry sectors, including refining, chemical processing, and power generation.</p></p>
