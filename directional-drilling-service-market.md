<p><p>Directional drilling service market types include the conventional system and rotary steerable system. In the conventional system, a bottom hole assembly is pushed through the ground using a series of connected pipes, allowing for controlled drilling in various directions. The rotary steerable system, on the other hand, uses advanced technology to accurately steer the drilling process by rotating the drill bit. Both systems offer efficient and precise drilling techniques for different applications in industries such as oil and gas, mining, and construction.</p></p>
