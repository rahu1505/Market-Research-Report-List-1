<p><p>An AWD (All-Wheel Drive) transfer case is a part of an AWD system that distributes power between the front and rear wheels of a vehicle. The transfer case can be found in vehicles like SUVs, pickup trucks, and off-road vehicles.</p><p>The AWD transfer case market is expected to witness significant growth in the coming years. <p><p>The AWD transfer case market consists of two main types: electronic and manual. Electronic transfer cases use advanced electronic control systems to engage and disengage the AWD system automatically based on various factors like wheel slippage and road conditions. This allows for seamless and hands-free operation. On the other hand, manual transfer cases require manual engagement and disengagement by the driver using a lever or switch. Although less advanced, manual transfer cases provide the driver with more control and can be more reliable in certain situations.</p></p>
