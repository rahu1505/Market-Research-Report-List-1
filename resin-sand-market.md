<p><ul><li>Resin Coated Sand</li><li>Resin Bonded Sand</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Resin sand market types include resin coated sand and resin bonded sand. Resin coated sand is a mixture of silica sand and resin that is used to produce cores and molds for metal casting. It provides better surface finish, dimensional accuracy, and high collapsibility. On the other hand, resin bonded sand involves mixing resin with sand and heating it to form a solid bond. This type of sand is commonly used in foundries for making molds and cores that require high strength and durability.</p></p>
