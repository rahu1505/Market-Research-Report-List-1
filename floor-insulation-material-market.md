<p><p>The floor insulation material market is categorized into various types. EPS (expanded polystyrene) and XPS (extruded polystyrene) are commonly used due to their thermal insulation properties. RockWool, made from volcanic rock, has excellent fire resistance. PIR/PU (polyisocyanurate/polyurethane) insulation offers high thermal resistance. GlassWool, made from recycled glass, provides good acoustic insulation. Other materials such as cork and natural fibers are also used. These materials are all employed to enhance energy efficiency, reduce heat loss, and improve comfort in buildings.</p></p>
