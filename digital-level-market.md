<p><p>The digital level market comprises two main types: vertical digital level and horizontal digital level. Vertical digital levels are primarily used to measure vertical angles or to establish deviations from the vertical reference. They find applications in construction, surveying, and architectural projects. On the other hand, horizontal digital levels are utilized to measure horizontal angles or to determine deviations from the horizontal reference. They are commonly employed in alignment tasks, such as verifying the levelness of surfaces or ensuring the accuracy of installations. Both types of digital levels are essential tools in various industries requiring precise measurements.</p></p>
