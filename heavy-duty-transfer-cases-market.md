<p><strong>The Heavy-Duty Transfer Cases Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gear Driven Transfer Cases</li><li>Chain Driven Transfer Cases</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The heavy-duty transfer cases market comprises two main types: gear-driven transfer cases and chain-driven transfer cases. Gear-driven transfer cases use a system of gears to transfer power from the transmission to the front and rear axles. They offer a higher torque capacity and are commonly used in heavy-duty trucks and off-road vehicles. On the other hand, chain-driven transfer cases use a chain to transfer power, which provides a smoother and quieter operation. These are often found in lighter-duty trucks and SUVs. Both types play a crucial role in distributing power to all wheels for improved traction and performance.</p></p>
