<p><p>Silicone-based defoamers are chemical additives used to control or eliminate foam during industrial processes. Foam is undesirable in many industries such as food processing, wastewater treatment, pulp and paper manufacturing, and oil and gas production as it can hinder the efficiency of the process and lead to product defects. Silicone-based defoamers are known for their low surface tension and ability to spread rapidly across the foam, breaking down the bubbles and reducing their stability.</p><p>The silicone-based defoamer market is projected to grow at a CAGR of 7% during the forecast period. This growth can be attributed to several factors. These defoamers are preferred over their alternatives due to their low toxicity and high efficiency.</p><p>A key trend observed in the silicone-based defoamer market is the increasing focus on product development to enhance performance and meet the specific requirements of end-use industries.
