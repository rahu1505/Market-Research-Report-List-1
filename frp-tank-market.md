<p><p>FRP tanks refer to tanks made of Fiber Reinforced Plastic, which are commonly used to store various liquids. The market for FRP tanks can be categorized into three main types based on their materials: glass fiber, carbon fiber, and vinylester. Glass fiber tanks are constructed using glass fibers as reinforcement, while carbon fiber tanks utilize carbon fibers for added strength. Vinylester tanks are manufactured using vinylester resin, known for its excellent corrosion resistance properties. These different types provide customers with options to choose the most suitable tank for their specific storage needs.</p></p>
