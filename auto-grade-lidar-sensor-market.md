<p><p>Auto Grade LiDAR sensors are used in autonomous vehicles to accurately perceive the surrounding environment. Mechanical LiDAR systems utilize moving parts to emit laser beams and measure their reflection, offering a larger range and higher accuracy. Solid-state LiDAR systems, on the other hand, are built without any moving parts, and instead, rely on advanced optics and semiconductor components to emit and detect laser beams. Solid-state LiDAR sensors are smaller, more durable, and potentially less expensive, making them ideal for mass production and integration in autonomous vehicles.</p></p>
