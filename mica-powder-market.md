<p><strong>The Mica Powder Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wet Ground</li><li>Dry Ground</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Mica powder is categorized into two main types: wet ground and dry ground. Wet ground mica powder is produced through a grinding process involving water, which helps to reduce the size of mica particles and create a smooth, glossy product. This type is commonly used in cosmetics, paints, and plastics. On the other hand, dry ground mica powder is produced without the use of water, and the resulting particles are coarser and have a glittery appearance. It is mainly used in decorative purposes, such as in arts and crafts or as an additive in candles and soaps.</p></p>
